Featured Artist: Chris Stapleton

Chris Stapleton has established himself as one of the most respected and beloved artists in country music today. With his powerful, soulful voice and masterful songwriting, Stapleton has breathed new life into the genre while honoring its rich traditions.

Having written numerous hit songs for other artists before stepping into the spotlight himself, Stapleton's debut solo album "Traveller" (2015) earned critical acclaim and multiple Grammy Awards, cementing his place in country music history.
